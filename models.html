Explore the Warhammer 40k Model Range

Warhammer 40k is not only known for its epic battles but also for its highly detailed models. Whether you're a painter, collector, or player, these miniatures bring the universe to life on the battlefield. Explore the various factions and their iconic models below.

Featured Models by Faction

Space Marines

The Space Marines are genetically engineered super-soldiers equipped with the best armor and weapons. Some iconic models include:

  • Intercessor Squad: A solid core troop choice with a balance of firepower and durability.
  • Primaris Redemptor Dreadnought: A towering war machine, packing heavy firepower and crushing melee strength.
  • Captain in Gravis Armor: A powerful commander with strong offensive and defensive capabilities.
See more Space Marines models

Chaos space Marines

Chaos Space Marines are traitors who have turned to the dark gods of Chaos. Their units are both terrifying and destructive, such as:

  • Chaos Lord: A brutal leader infused with the dark powers of the Warp.
  • Chaos Cultists: Fervent followers of Chaos, they overwhelm enemies with their sheer numbers.
  • Daemon Prince: Once mortal, these terrifying figures have been granted immense power by the Chaos Gods.
See more Chaos models

Necrons

The Necrons are an ancient race of skeletal robots, armed with advanced technology and indomitable will:

  • Necron Warriors: Basic foot soldiers with Gauss weapons that strip matter at the atomic level.
  • Canoptek Wraiths: Fast, floating constructs that phase through walls to strike at their enemies.
  • Doomsday Ark: A floating fortress armed with devastating weapons capable of destroying entire armies.
